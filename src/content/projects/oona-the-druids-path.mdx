## Overview

Oona the Druid’s Path is a published 2.5D platformer developed in Unreal Engine 4 by a multidisciplinary team. The project is valuable in the portfolio because it demonstrates gameplay implementation inside a team pipeline, with movement, combat, collectibles and enemy behaviours interacting with level design needs.

## My contribution

- Implemented character movement mechanics and responsive player actions.
- Built jump / dash behaviour, down attack and other player-facing gameplay features.
- Developed multiple enemy behaviours and AI elements.
- Worked on collectibles and gameplay objects.
- Contributed level design support, including arena work and platform movement utilities.

## Technical focus

The case study should emphasize practical gameplay implementation: shipping player movement and enemy behaviours that need to feel responsive, support level design and integrate with animation, perception and combat logic.
